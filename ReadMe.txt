Our broad idea is based on application of AI in the field of medical and healthcare. We used a bot to take input from user such as symptoms experienced by the user and provide the probable disease experienced by the patient, in light of the diagnosis, appropriate remedy advice are provided and if doctor consultancy is required or not. By using this chat bot people who tends to avoid crowd or who aren't able to access hospital services in emergency or those who don't have much knowledge regarding diseases and symptoms can get a general idea and it may prove fruitful in times of urgency or worst situation.
